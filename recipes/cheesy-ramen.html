Cheesy Ramen

Description

A simple ramen dish with a touch of cheese to give it a different taste.

Ingredients

  • 2 cups water
  • 1 (3 ounce) package any flavor ramen noodles
  • 1 slice American cheese

Instructions

  1. Bring water to a boil in a saucepan. Add ramen noodles and cook until tender, about 2 minutes.
  2. Pour out water, then stir in seasoning packet and cheese until well blended.
